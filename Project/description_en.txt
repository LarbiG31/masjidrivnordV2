Masjid Riv Nord's vision is to build a permanent and dignified place of worship for the growing Muslim community of Saint-Jérôme.

Our current mosque can no longer accommodate all worshippers, especially during Friday prayers and religious celebrations. This building project represents much more than a structure — it is a spiritual home, a community centre, and an educational space for future generations.

With your support, we can make this dream a reality together. Every donation, large or small, brings us closer to our goal.

May Allah reward you all.
